FAQ

Questions? Got you.

Does it slow down my browser? +
Nope. The animation runs on a lightweight canvas overlay that doesn't interfere with the page at all. You won't notice any performance difference.
Will the website owner see my overlay? +
No. The overlay only exists in your browser. It's injected on top of the page locally — nobody else sees it, and nothing is sent to the website.
Can I use it on any website? +
Yes, any website. You assign profiles to specific domains and it fires automatically every time you visit. Some heavily secured sites (like banking) may block extensions — but most sites work perfectly.
How does the Pro license work? +
After purchasing on Gumroad you'll receive a license key by email. Enter it in the extension settings and Pro unlocks instantly. It's a one-time purchase — no subscription, no expiry.
What happens when I get future updates? +
Pro users get all updates at a small upgrade price rather than full price. Free users always keep access to the free tier features.
Can I use it to prank someone? +
100% yes and we fully encourage it. Set up a profile with "GET OUT" or "HELL NAH" in Impact font, assign it to their favorite site, and hand them your laptop. You're welcome.
Does it work on Chrome only? +
Currently Chrome only — but it also works on Brave, Edge, and any other Chromium-based browser. Firefox support is coming soon.
